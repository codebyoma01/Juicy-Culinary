Common Questions

FAQs

How far in advance should I order? +

We recommend at least 5–7 days for standard cakes and a minimum of 3 weeks for wedding cakes. For large-scale or very intricate designs, 4–6 weeks is ideal to ensure the best result.

Do you offer tastings? +

Yes! For wedding cakes, we offer a tasting session by appointment. Reach out via WhatsApp to schedule yours. A small fee applies which is deducted from your final order.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept bank transfers to all major Nigerian banks. A 50% deposit is required to confirm your order, with the balance paid before delivery or pickup.

Can I provide my own design or inspiration photo? +

Absolutely! Share any inspiration images via WhatsApp or on the order form. We'll work with your vision and suggest enhancements where possible. The final cake is always unique to you.
